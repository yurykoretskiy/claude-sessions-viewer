POC — global search over sessions

Left panel mocks the Search view that would sit above the sessions tree in the Claude Sessions sidebar. Right pane mocks the existing conversation viewer.

Try it: type hermes board, docker or telegram. Exact-phrase match, live while typing.

Results are grouped by session, newest session first, most recent session pre-expanded. Each snippet = who said it, when, and ~100 chars of context around the hit.

Click a snippet — the viewer opens that session, scrolls to the exact message and flashes it. That's the handoff to your normal "glide over the session" reading.

Toggle tool output to see how much noise tool results add — that's why they're excluded by default.

Data is a hand-made subset modelled on the real Hermes sessions, 14 Jul 2026.
